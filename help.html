Connect Four is a strategy game to drop pieces in a gameboard and connect four pieces of your color.

  1. Strategize a great place to drop your piece.
  2. Move your mouse over the column in which you wish to release your precious circular object.
  3. Click on the left mouse button to commit your mastery plan.
  4. Repeat steps 1-3 for the next player.
